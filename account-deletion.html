Zajedno: Account Deletion and Data Policy

Last Updated: October 11, 2025

We respect your right to privacy and complete control over your data. If you decide that you no longer wish to use the "Zajedno" (Together) application, we have provided you with a simple and secure way to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

We are sorry to see you considering leaving and hope you will give us another chance in the future.

How to Delete Your Account?

There are two ways you can request the deletion of your account. We recommend using the in-app method as it is the fastest and most secure.

Method 1: In-App Account Deletion (Recommended)

This is the fastest way to permanently delete your account.

  1. Open the Zajedno app.
  2. Go to your Profile (bottom right corner).
  3. Find and open Settings.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the page and select Delete Account.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions and confirm your decision.

Warning: This action is permanent and irreversible. Once you confirm deletion, your account and data cannot be recovered.

Method 2: Email Request

If for any reason you cannot access the application, you can send a deletion request to our support team.

  1. Send an email to: support@salamanderapp.org
  2. In the email subject line, write: Account Deletion Request.
  3. In the email body, please provide the following information so we can verify your identity:
    • Email address you used to register in the app.
    • Your first and last name as stated in your profile.
    • Your date of birth.

Our team will process your request as soon as possible after successful identity verification.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our servers:

  • Your complete profile (name, age, city, description, etc.).
  • All your photos.
  • All your conversations and messages.
  • List of your matches and likes (given and received).
  • Your wallet balance (all remaining points).
  • All history of your interactions (swipes, blocks).
  • Your search preferences.

Data Retention

For security, legal, and regulatory reasons, we retain a limited set of data in anonymized form (without the ability to link to your identity) for a period of 90 days after account deletion. This data includes:

  • Internal, anonymized user identifier.
  • Records of reports you submitted or reports submitted against you, for abuse prevention purposes.
  • Transaction history (purchases), without personal data, for financial reporting.

After the 90-day period expires, this data is also permanently deleted.

Confirmation

We emphasize once again that the account deletion process is irreversible. If you decide to return to "Zajedno" in the future, you will need to create a completely new account.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us at support@salamanderapp.org.
