What players can do

Juggle Muggle is designed for short, repeatable sessions with clear rewards and fair competitive play. Players can practice, chase high scores, unlock cosmetics, and keep their progress synced with a simple username account.

Skill-based gameplay

Tap to keep the ball alive, chain combos, hit perfect touches, and improve personal bests across classic, timed, rush, and challenge style modes.

Progression and cosmetics

Earn coins and XP, claim daily streak rewards, complete achievements, and unlock balls, trails, and field themes from the in-game locker.

Leaderboards and sync

Compare weekly, monthly, and all-time scores. Optional username sign-in can back up profile, stats, unlocks, and progress.

Support and safety

Juggle Muggle keeps the experience focused on gameplay while giving players clear support, purchase, privacy, and deletion information.

Player experience

Account access
Players may play as guests. They can optionally create a username/password account to restore progress.
User content
The game does not include public chat, photo sharing, or open user-generated content posting.
Purchases
In-app purchases may unlock premium cosmetics, starter rewards, or remove advertising where ads are enabled.
Permissions
The game needs internet access for cloud services and may ask for notifications only if the user opts in.
