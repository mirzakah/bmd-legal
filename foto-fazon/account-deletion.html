FotoFazon account deletion

Last updated: June 12, 2026

You can request deletion of your FotoFazon account and associated personal data inside the FotoFazon app when the account settings screen is available:

  1. Open FotoFazon.
  2. Go to Account or Settings.
  3. Open Privacy, Data, or Account Controls.
  4. Choose Request account deletion or Delete account.

If you cannot access the app, submit a deletion request from this page using the same email address you used for FotoFazon.

If the form does not open your email client, send the same information directly to support@fotofazon.app with the subject "FotoFazon account deletion".

How deletion works

FotoFazon will verify account ownership before processing deletion. We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days.

Deletion may remove or anonymize

  • Firebase account profile and authentication identifiers.
  • Uploaded photos and challenge entries where deletion is technically and legally available.
  • Votes, rankings, reports, moderation records, and comments tied to the account where possible.
  • Device push tokens and notification preferences.
  • RevenueCat app user linkage and entitlement association where applicable.

Some records may be retained

We may retain limited records when required for security, fraud prevention, legal, tax, billing, refund, dispute resolution, moderation, abuse prevention, or audit integrity of challenges and purchases.

For more details, read the FotoFazon Privacy Policy.
