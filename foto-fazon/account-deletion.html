FotoFazon account deletion

Last updated: June 13, 2026

You can request deletion of your FotoFazon account and associated personal data inside the FotoFazon app:

  1. Open FotoFazon.
  2. Go to Profile > Settings > Delete account.
  3. Review the deletion notice and confirm the request.

If you cannot access the app, submit a deletion request from this page using an account identifier we can verify. If you signed in with email or a social login, include that login identifier. If you used an anonymous account, include your Firebase UID or public handle.

If the form does not open your email client, send the same information directly to support@fotofazon.app with the subject "FotoFazon account deletion". Include your account identifier, contact email, and whether the account uses email, social login, or anonymous Firebase sign-in.

How deletion works

FotoFazon will verify account ownership before processing deletion. We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days.

Deletion may remove or anonymize

  • Firebase account profile and authentication identifiers.
  • Uploaded photos and challenge entries associated with the account.
  • Votes, rankings, comments, reports, and public handles tied to the account where possible.
  • Device push tokens and notification preferences.
  • RevenueCat app user linkage and entitlement association where applicable.
  • Support messages connected to the deletion request, unless retention is required.

Some records may be retained

We may retain limited records when required for security, fraud prevention, purchase verification, tax or accounting obligations, legal compliance, refunds, dispute resolution, moderation and abuse prevention, or audit integrity of challenges and purchases.

Subscriptions and store billing

Deleting a FotoFazon account does not cancel subscriptions or billing managed by Apple App Store or Google Play. If you have an active FotoFazon Plus subscription, cancel it separately in your App Store or Google Play account settings.

For more details, read the FotoFazon Privacy Policy.
