Community Rules

Last updated: June 13, 2026

FotoFazon is a user-generated photo challenge community for restoration, colorization, parody edits, voting, and rankings. These rules apply to photos, captions, prompts, AI edits, manual edits, votes, reports, profile information, and any other content submitted through FotoFazon.

1. Upload only content you can use

  • Upload photos you own or have permission to submit.
  • Respect privacy, publicity, copyright, trademark, and family rights.
  • Do not upload private documents, addresses, phone numbers, financial data, or other sensitive personal information.
  • Do not expose someone else's private photo or identity without permission.

2. Keep people safe

  • No sexual content involving minors, child exploitation, grooming, or sexualized depictions of people who appear under 18.
  • No non-consensual intimate imagery, sexual harassment, threats, blackmail, stalking, or doxxing.
  • No hateful, abusive, degrading, or targeted harassment based on protected characteristics.
  • No encouragement of self-harm, violence, dangerous behavior, fraud, or illegal activity.

3. Label and use AI edits responsibly

AI-generated or AI-edited outputs are labeled when AI tools are enabled. Do not use restoration, colorization, or parody tools to impersonate people, mislead viewers, fabricate evidence, sexualize someone, exploit tragedy, or create realistic harmful depictions.

4. Parody and humor still have limits

Parody entries may be playful, exaggerated, or satirical, but they must not become harassment, hate, non-consensual sexual content, rights violations, or deceptive impersonation. Challenge hosts and moderators may remove parody content that creates safety, legal, or store-policy risk.

5. Fair voting and rankings

  • Do not buy, sell, automate, or coordinate votes.
  • Do not use multiple accounts, scripts, or abuse reports to manipulate rankings.
  • Do not interfere with Firebase, RevenueCat, store billing, credits, subscriptions, reports, moderation, or app infrastructure.

6. Reporting and moderation

Users can report content or accounts in the app where available, or contact support at support@fotofazon.app. FotoFazon may review, restrict, remove, down-rank, disable, or retain content as needed for user safety, legal compliance, store rules, abuse prevention, dispute handling, or challenge integrity.

7. Enforcement

Breaking these rules may lead to content removal, ranking changes, loss of challenge eligibility, account restrictions, purchase or credit review, suspension, or deletion where allowed by law and store rules. Severe safety issues may be escalated to platform providers or appropriate authorities.
