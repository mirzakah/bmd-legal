What FotoFazon does

FotoFazon lets users submit old or personal photos into themed challenges, apply AI-powered edits, and participate in community voting with reporting and moderation tools.

Creative photo challenges

Users can upload photos, join challenge prompts, vote on entries, and view rankings. Uploaded content remains subject to reporting, moderation, and removal when it violates the rules.

AI edits and labels

The app may restore, colorize, enhance, or create parody-style edits. AI-generated or AI-edited outputs are labeled so users and reviewers understand when content was altered.

Purchases and accounts

RevenueCat manages credit packs and FotoFazon Plus subscriptions. Firebase supports authentication, app data, media storage, notifications, and push token handling.
