Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

FotoFazon, listed on the App Store as Foto Fazon, is a creative photo challenge app for uploading old or personal photos, manual edits, voting, rankings, moderation, and optional AI or purchase features when enabled. This policy is practical store-review information and is not a substitute for legal advice.

1. Data FotoFazon collects

  • Account data: Firebase user ID, email address, display name, sign-in provider, and authentication metadata.
  • User-generated content: uploaded photos, challenge entries, captions, prompts, votes, rankings, reports, moderation decisions, and AI-edited outputs when AI features are enabled.
  • AI edit data when enabled: source image references, edit type, restoration, colorization, enhancement, parody parameters, safety labels, and generated output references.
  • Purchase data when enabled: RevenueCat app user ID, entitlements, credit balances, product IDs, subscription status, purchase history, and restore-purchase state.
  • Device and notification data: app instance data, device platform, push tokens, notification preferences, and delivery metadata.
  • Diagnostics: app version, build number, crash data, logs, and device/runtime information used to improve reliability.
  • Support data: messages sent to support@fotofazon.app, account identifiers, screenshots, and issue details users choose to provide.

2. How data is used

  • To create accounts and authenticate users.
  • To upload, store, process, and display challenge photos.
  • To provide AI restoration, colorization, enhancement, and parody edits when enabled.
  • To label AI-edited content and support content-safety review.
  • To operate voting, rankings, challenges, reports, and moderation.
  • To process RevenueCat purchases, credits, subscriptions, and restores when enabled.
  • To send notifications when users opt in or when app events require delivery.
  • To respond to support, privacy, deletion, and billing questions.

3. Third-party processors

  • Firebase: authentication, app data, storage, functions, messaging, and diagnostics.
  • RevenueCat: in-app purchases, subscriptions, credits, entitlements, and restore state when purchase features are enabled.
  • Apple App Store and Google Play: app distribution, purchase billing, refunds, and subscription management.
  • AI processing providers: image restoration, colorization, enhancement, parody generation, and safety labeling when AI features are enabled.
  • Apple Push Notification service and Firebase Cloud Messaging: push notification delivery.

4. User-generated content and moderation

FotoFazon includes user-generated photo entries, votes, reports, and rankings. Users should upload only photos they have the right to use. Reported content may be reviewed, restricted, removed, or preserved as limited evidence for safety, abuse prevention, or dispute handling.

5. AI edits and safety labels

When AI features are enabled, AI-edited images may look realistic. FotoFazon labels AI-generated or AI-edited outputs and may restrict content that impersonates, deceives, sexualizes, harasses, exploits, or violates applicable laws, platform rules, or challenge rules.

6. Purchases and restore

When purchase features are enabled, FotoFazon uses RevenueCat for credit packs and FotoFazon Plus subscriptions. Product IDs include ff_credits_small, ff_credits_medium, ff_credits_large, ff_plus_monthly, and ff_plus_yearly. Users can restore purchases in the app. Billing, cancellation, and refunds are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play.

7. Data retention

Account, content, ranking, purchase, and moderation records are kept while needed to provide the app, maintain challenge integrity, process purchases, protect users, and meet security, legal, tax, billing, or dispute obligations. Deleted or removed content may persist in backups for a limited period.

8. User controls

  • Users can report content or accounts from the app where available.
  • Users can restore purchases from the app purchase screen when purchases are enabled.
  • Users can request account deletion in the app or by using the web form.
  • If app access is unavailable, users can request deletion at /foto-fazon/account-deletion.html.
