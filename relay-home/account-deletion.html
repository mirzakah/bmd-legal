RelayHome account deletion

Last updated: June 11, 2026

You can request deletion of your RelayHome account and associated personal data inside the RelayHome app:

  1. Open RelayHome.
  2. Go to Account or Settings.
  3. Open Privacy & Security.
  4. Choose Request account deletion or Delete account.

In the current app, open Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Request account deletion. We are adding Account -> Delete account as a shorter path.

If you cannot access the app, submit a deletion request from this page using the same email address you used for RelayHome.

If the form does not open your email client, send the same information directly to support@salamanderapp.org with the subject "RelayHome account deletion".

How deletion works

RelayHome will verify account ownership before processing deletion. We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days.

Deletion may remove or anonymize

  • Account profile.
  • Household membership.
  • Device push tokens.
  • Personal identifiers tied to tasks and routines where possible.
  • Subscription linkage where applicable.

Some records may be retained

We may retain limited records when required for security, fraud prevention, legal, tax, billing, dispute resolution, or audit integrity of household events.

For more details, read the RelayHome Privacy Policy.
