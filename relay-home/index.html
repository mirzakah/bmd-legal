What RelayHome does

RelayHome is a shared household operations app for handoffs, routines, timers, queues, focus mode, household subjects, and subscriptions.

Household handoffs

Create or join a household, invite trusted members, start a relay from templates, and move the next step to the right person.

Queues and timers

Track personal queues, active timers, focus work, boomerang reasons, and household timelines with calm status updates.

Privacy-first MVP

No contacts, location, photos, videos, audio, calendar, health data, advertising ID, ads, or third-party tracking in the MVP.
