RelayHome Support

Last updated: June 11, 2026

For RelayHome support, contact support@salamanderapp.org. Include your account email, device type, app version if available, and a short description of the issue.

If you are reviewing RelayHome, use the credentials provided in App Store Connect or Play Console App access instructions.

Common support topics

  • Login or magic link issues.
  • Apple or Google sign-in.
  • Creating or joining a household.
  • Invite links and invite codes.
  • Starting a relay from templates.
  • Task handoff, Boomerang, timers, and Focus Mode.
  • Push notifications not arriving.
  • Subscriptions, restore purchases, and billing issues.
  • Privacy, export, and account deletion.
